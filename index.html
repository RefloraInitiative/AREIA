Understanding the Impact

As a hub for contemporary products and unique fashion brands in the international market, we seek an ongoing understanding of our impact. This process has brought us to fully acknowledge the carbon emissions from our operations - and to take action.

Our journey through sustainability has encountered in nature-based solutions a way of expanding our engagement. By investing in extraordinary, certified, and high-quality forest projects we are able to balance our carbon footprint and create impactful partnerships.

Agrocortex Impact report

Harmonizing the Relationship between Fashion and Forest

With Agrocortex, we are creating tangible impact in Brazil's forest land

We decided to invest in Agrocortex after learning that besides committing to forest conservation, the project also develops an important relationship with local artisans in the Amazon region. That is what we believe fashion is about!

As part of its activities, the project donates wood to the artisans' association in Acre - part of the Amazon region - stimulating the production of handicrafts. The program helps 40 craftsmen, indirectly benefiting 250 families. This activity represents the main source of income for more than 50% of the artisans' families in the state. In addition, parts of the land property were donated to local community families, and 25 new landowners are currently in the process of legalizing their land.
