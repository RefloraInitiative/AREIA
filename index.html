Sustainability

Fashion is the utmost form of human expression.
At AREIA we understand that committing to sustainability is an integral part of our approach to innovative curatorship.
That means taking action as part of the fashion industry. 

"Understanding the impact of our operations has led us to take action."
As a hub for contemporary products and unique fashion brands in the international market, we seek an ongoing understanding of our impact. 
This process has brought us to fully acknowledge the carbon emissions from our operations - and to take action.
Our journey through sustainability has encountered in nature-based solutions a way of expanding our engagement. By investing in extraordinary, certified, and high-quality forest projects we are able to balance our carbon footprint and create impactful partnerships.
AGROCORTEX FOREST PROJECT
We decided to invest in Agrocortex after learning that besides its commitment to forest conservation, they also have an important relationship with local artisans in the Amazon region. 
That's what we believe fashion is about!
Winner of the prize for the best project in the Voluntary Carbon Market in 2020, Agrocortex symbolizes a new relationship between society and nature.
Fighting against illegal logging, and positioning itself as an important player in Sustainable Forest Management, the project demonstrates that progress and sustainability are possible. 
By using a multi-purpose perspective, Agrocortex reduces the impact of wood extraction, generates income, and positively impacts the local community and the environment.
As part of its activities, the project donates wood to the artisans' association in Acre - part of the Amazon region - stimulating the production of handicrafts. The program helps 40 craftsmen, indirectly benefiting 250 families. This activity represents the main source of income for more than 50% of the artisans' families in the state.
In addition, parts of the land property were donated to local community families, and 25 new landowners are currently in the process of legalizing their land.
